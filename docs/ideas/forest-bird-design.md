## What Are Birds?

Birds are **messengers** in the Forest. When an agent (like James or Ellie) needs information from a different tree, they send a bird to go get it. The bird flies over, finds what they need, and brings it back ‚Äî all without the agent having to stop their work and wait.

Think of it like sending a text message and getting a notification when someone replies, instead of standing there waiting for the answer.
